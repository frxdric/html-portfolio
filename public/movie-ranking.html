Fredric's Top Movies

Drama/Horror

The Pianist

A World War II film about a jewish pianist named Wladyslaw. The film follows his struggles navigating through this time period and relying on music to bring peace to him during the Holocaust. It's a story about resilience amidst overwhelming adversities.

Saw X

The entire franchise is full of plot twists and brutality. Saw X is the fifth installment in the film series. A twisted mastermind, "Jigsaw" (also known as John), challenges people to survive gruesome traps to atone for their sins. In Saw X, a group of cons pull a trick on the mastermind, causing them to be kidnapped and forced to undergo the challenges to determine their fate as a consequence.

Crooklyn

A classic film directed by Spike Lee. This film was set in the 70s in Brooklyn. It follows the story of a young girl named Troy and captures the ups and downs African-Americans face. Exploring family dynamics, community, and family drama, this film is special because it shows how one family navigates through poverty while still finding time for fun and humor. It's a great film and story.
